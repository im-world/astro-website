Intra IITI Tech Meet 2019
"Exoplanet Detection"

Event Conducted by: Saurabh Kumar, Danyal Shahid Shamsi, Devashish Kaushik, Shiv Kumar Prashant Pareek, Ribhu Das Purkayastha, Dibyanshu Pranjal, Hans Raj

Previous Next


  • Date: 16th,19th,20th October 2019
  • Venue: 1E 205



Highlights -


  • 3 day workshop cum Hackathon focusing on detection of exoplanets from space telescope data
  • Attendant skills, such as basic programming with Python, basic data science and basic theory of stellar systems and exoplanet detection was covered
  • Attendees included students from Btech, Masters and PhD.
  • The workshop was presented with an interactive Jupyter tutorial and slides with hyperlinks to videos so that people gets hands on experience on everything.

Details -


  • Day 1: Basic Python, Numpy, Matplotlib
    Day 1 went great especially for those who were new to python, we tried to get their hands dirty with everything thing in python from identifiers to functions followed by visual representation in form of different graphs using matplotlib and various array operations in Numpy.
  • Day 2: Exoplanet theory, Kepler telescope, FITS file handling, Astropy, applied machine learning and data processing
    Day 2 was important for the ones who wanted a deeper insight of what we can do with computers using simple algorithms. From basics to an introduction to neural networks, people learnt the application of ML and how to apply various libraries for the same.We also presented the theory of how Transit Method works and how it is used to find various properties of an exoplanet.The workshop ended with some insights of the implementation of Exoplanet.
  • Day 3: Hackathon
    Day 3 was important as people used their skills, learnt in the previous days and tried to find the properties of exoplanet right way. But this project is not an easy thing to crack in terms of programming so the submission time has been extended to 10 days so that everyone can give their best.
